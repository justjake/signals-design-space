The design keeps cached newest state in K0 and records speculative dependencies in an edge-only K1. Every React-mode write enters an ordered replay ledger before updating K0. Each render pass evaluates non-newest values in isolated scratch storage that survives yields but is never reused by another pass. A union reach walk provides transitive invalidation and synchronous writer-context delivery without cross-write dedup. Exact full-token clock snapshots validate only Suspense/error capsules, while the fork supplies classification, yield/resume, root lock-in, retirement, async attribution, and lane-scoped scheduling.
